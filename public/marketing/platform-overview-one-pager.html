Same scoring engine, tuned for executive communications — tracking the CEO's personal LinkedIn and X presence alongside corporate channels.

Monitors article health and search visibility — critical because Wikipedia often ranks first when investors, journalists, or employees search for Pfizer or its leadership.

Wikipedia is an open-source platform — it cannot be updated or edited by Pfizer directly. Changes must come from the volunteer community that runs Wikipedia.
